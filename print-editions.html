Print Editions

Each spring, a beautifully designed volume — for the shelf, for the desk, for the long read.

Articles Series Print Editions

Our print edition is designed by student editors and printed in a limited annual run. It is the form of the journal we care most about — a physical object that rewards slow reading, that can be lent and borrowed and left on a shelf. Each volume has a theme that gathers its contents without reducing them.
